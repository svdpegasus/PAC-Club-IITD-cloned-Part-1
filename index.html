THE PHYSICS & ASTRONOMY CLUB
IIT DELHI
Through this club, we aim to create an engaging environment where scientific thought and logical
reasoning is encouraged to tackle both previously solved and unsolved questions regarding the
universe and the laws of physics.

We focus on gathering enthusiasts and facilitating knowledge sharing across all groups of
students and to capture the interest of all the students in the mysteries of the universe. We also
promote learning from the experts of the research field and the people in the industry.
